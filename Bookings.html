BOOK A SEAT

If you click on the link provided below, you can make a booking for one of our prestigous exhibitions.

You can view the available time-slots and reserve yourself a seat. The bookings can be made a month in advance, and a limited time only same day reservations can be made. The tickets have the following perks:

* Reserved Entry time to center * Special early screening for permanent collections and special exhibitions.

After clicking on the "BOOKING" button you will be be taken to a page where you can slect from the available time-slots. You may secure a free ticket 14days before the exhibition. As we are heading into a much busy (and Warmer) festive season, the best way to avoid waiting in line is to conserve your free gallery tickets before you arrive. We in high hopes that the latest, same day ticket option makes preparing your visit more convinient than ever. All guests will be required to provide information relevant to contact tracing purposes due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
