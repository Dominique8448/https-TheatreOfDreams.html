As noteworthy as the art collection is at the Theatre of Dreams, the center in addition to this boasts a spectecular collection of architecture. The three architects who have collaborated in the design of the center, J.N Katupose, T.M. Sitamulaho and R.C.G Skrywer, are among the greatest names in architecture of the 21st century. Though each offers a very alternate style and period of modern architecture, their combined efforts, starting with Katupose’s original Glass stone building, followed in July 2020 by Sitamulaho’s bushhammered concrete addition, and Skrywer’s three-part clad porcelain and granite addition in October 2020, have resulted in a unique architectural achievement.
